The Six Values

Through our questionare, we gather data to understand what you value in the stocks you invest in. Here is what we ask you to consider.

Environmental

How much do you value a company caring about the natural world? This includes issues such as climate change, pollution, resource depletion, deforestation, and biodiversity loss.

Social

How much do you value a company actively managing their local and national communities. Social criteria used in ESG analysis may include issues such as human rights, labor practices, product safety, customer privacy, and community relations.

Governance

How much do you care about companies monitoring their internal structure? This may include issues such as board composition, executive compensation, shareholder rights, and risk management practices.

Controversy

How much do you care about a company's involvement in controversial issues? This may include issues such as weapons, tobacco, alcohol, gambling, and fossil fuels.

Gender Diversity

How important is it that a company is diverse with respect to gender? We evaluate companies based on the ratio of male and female executives.

Racial Diversity

How important is it that a company is diverse with respect to race? We evaluate companies based on the proportion of minority employees.

The Algorithm

We use a propritary algorithm to take your answers and compare it to our private database which analyzes stocks from the Standard and Poor's 500 Index. The image on the left shows this data plotted with respect to three variables. We then select stocks that are closest to your answers in the multidimensional space.

How much to buy?

Once we have selected the stocks, we must decide how much of each to buy. This process is known as asset or weight allocation. To determine the optimal allocation, we use the award-winning Markowitzian Theory, more specifically, the idea of an Efficient Frontier, shown on the left. We then use Mean-variance optimization theory to find a portfolio made from your basket of stocks on the efficient frontier.
